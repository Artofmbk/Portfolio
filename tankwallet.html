Client: TankWallet - Palo Alto, CA
Project: Price Cap Plan Marketing Materials
Role: Sales, Marketing, Graphic Design, Animation/Motion Graphics, Illustration
Tools: Pencil & Paper, Photoshop, Illustrator, After Effects

Tankwallet is a tech company in Palo Alto, CA that provides fuel cost saving solutions to both large scale companies and indivdual consumers. I was responsible for graphic design and animation materials created to educate consumers on the company's various products.


Above is the 30 sec "elevator pitch" explanation video we released to introduce the concept of a "Price Cap Plan." I wrote, illustrated, animated and cast the voice actors for each of these animations.


This video is a deeper dive into the mechanics of the company's Price Cap Plans. Targeted for companies in the trucking industry, the product was meant to help curb the cost of rising fuel prices for businesses that consumed vast quantities of fuel. This explanation video was the result of field testing we conducted to gauge public understanding of how TankWallet's Price Cap Plans work.


In addition to offering fuel plans to larger companies, TankWallet was also created for use by individual consumers. This brief video was created to demonstrate how the product works for those users as well.

Building up the characters from concept sketches to vector assets for the animation.

Early sketches for "Chuck" our iconic jolly truck driver. We wanted Chuck to be an approachable and friendly character so we decided to go with a less realistic and more cartoony look.

Early Storyboard for the "Price Cap Plan" explanation video.

A small sample of the vector assets made during the creation of the animations.

In addition to the animations, I also created digital marketing materials for the company's products. Below are a few examples created to be sent as emails to prospective clients demonstrating how TankWallet's Price Cap Plans performs over time using data obtained from the U.S. Department of Energy.

My time at TankWallet was an incredibly challenging and rewarding experience. Working in the fast-paced environment of a startup provided many challenging opportunies to adapt and grow as an artist and designer. During my time there I was able to write, storyboard, illustrate, animate, and edit several videos for the company.
