Much of my personal work has been an attempt to express various aspects of the profound spiritual experiences Iâ€™ve had in my life. I incorporate symbolism within my work to communicate personal psychological insights received during these experiences. Though my range as an artist is diverse, my style and aesthetic are very much rooted in who I am as a human being. I try to weave the surreal and the real by drawing dream like states as crisp photo realistic images. I believe most of us spend large portions of our lives sleep walking, failing to realize we are out of touch with our selves and those around us. Ironically, it is only when we wake up that we are actually able to make our dreams come true. For me, I feel most awake when I am making art.
