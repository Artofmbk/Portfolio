I attended the University of North Carolina at Asheville, where I received my Bachelor of Arts in Multimedia Arts and Sciences. I’m endlessly fascinated with the creative process, in whatever form it takes; my work spans across Traditional and Digital Illustration, Animation, Graphic Design, Woodworking, and Pyrography. Though my range as an artist is diverse, my style and aesthetic are very much rooted in who I am as a human being.

Much of my personal work has been an attempt to express various aspects of the profound spiritual experiences I’ve had in my life. I incorporate symbolism within my work to communicate personal psychological insights received through these experiences. I try to weave the surreal and the real by rendering dreamlike states into crisp photorealistic images. I believe most of us spend large portions of our lives sleep walking, failing to realize we are out of touch with our selves and the world around us. Ironically, it is only when we wake up that we are actually able to make our dreams come true. For me, I feel most awake when I am making art.
